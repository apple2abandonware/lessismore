HOW TO LOAD KEYS OF ACHERON

Boot up your HELLFIRE WARRIOR disk following the loading instructions
that came with your game. At the point where the computer asks you if
you want to re-enter a saved level, answer "y". You will be asked to
insert the disk with the saved level. At this time, insert KEYS OF
ACHERON and press the "Return" key. Enter a level number between 5 and 8
and hit the "Return" key. You are now ready to begin play.


NOTE: KEYS OF ACHERON has four levels STARTING at level 5.
