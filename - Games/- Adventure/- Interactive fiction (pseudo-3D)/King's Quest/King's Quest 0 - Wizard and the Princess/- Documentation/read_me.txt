Wizard and the Princess (1980), also known as Adventure in Serenia (1982), is an adventure
game by On-Line Systems and was the second title released in On-Line Systems' Hi-Res
Adventures series after Mystery House.

Wizard and the Princess is a prelude to the King's Quest series in both story and concept
(though chronologically set several years before King's Quest V). It was also the first
adventure game released with full color graphics.
