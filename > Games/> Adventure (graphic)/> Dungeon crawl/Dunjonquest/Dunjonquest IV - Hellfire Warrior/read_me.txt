Hellfire Warrior is the direct sequel to Temple of Apshai, leading
players into the ruins that are unearthed below the temple. The levels
are "The Lower Reaches of Apshai", "The Labyrinth" of the minotaur, "The
Vault of the Dead" and "The Plains of Hell".


NOTE: HELLFIRE WARRIOR has four levels STARTING at level 5.
When asked what level to play you should start on level 5.
