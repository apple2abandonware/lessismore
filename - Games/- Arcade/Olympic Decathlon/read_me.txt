Olympic Decathlon is a sports game written by Timothy W. Smith for the
TRS-80 and published in 1980 by Microsoft. It was ported to the Apple II
in 1981. The 1982 version for the IBM PC was renamed Microsoft
Decathlon.

Olympic Decathlon received the Creative Computing Game of the Year Award
at the 1980 West Coast Computer Faire, and is considered an important
and early contribution to sports themed computer games.
