Notes

If you have too many inventory objects, you can always drop an object and pick it up later.  No one will steal it, and it'll stay there forever.

When you die, you must choose a door. It then randomly quits the game, takes you back unharmed to where you were, or allows you another chance. Pure guesswork.

You can save and load your game using the "save" and "load" commands. You name your games with a one word description. If you forget the names, they're in the softporn directory as gamename.gam

Fun Things to Try in the Game

Even though you die, just for fun, try "eat"ing any of the girls in the game. (waitress in disco, blonde in casino, etc.)
